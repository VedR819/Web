SSHS Classes: Reviewed and Ranked

Everybody has their own preferences. They either love a class, hate a class, or are fine with it. I have a good understanding on which classes I like and which classes I do not like. Today, I will share my opinion on the classes I have, starting from least favorite to my most favorite class.

7. ACE

This class had to be at the bottom of my class tier list. This technically doesn't even count as a class. My teacher is super nice but you don't do much in that class. You just wait until your nest class starts.

6. P.E.

Besides from ACE, this is my least favorite class. This is because of two reasons. First, I don't like the teachers. They would say I did something wrong when I didn't do anything.

Second, we spend most of our time stretching instead of doing things like soccer or football. Other than that, this class is really solid.

5. English

I feel really bad for doing this, but it had to be done. The teacher (Mr. Shaprio) is a great teacher. He teaches well and his grading style is really nice.

However, the class is just boring. This has nothing to do with the teacher. English, overall, is a boring subject for me.

4. Spanish

The teacher is really nice in this class but I just don't like the subject. I feel like learning Spanish is a little pointless so that's why it's ranked so low. If this was ranked on teachers, this class would be on my Top 3 list.

3. Exploring Computer Science

This class is a solid class. Alot of my good friends are in this class and coding is fun in general. The teacher is also really nice.

The only reason this is lower than usual is not because the class is neccesarily bad, it's just that I think that there are better classes than this. Other than this, this class is great!

2. Math

I put that class at number two because I love math. The teacher is also really nice and makes the subject as intresting as possible.

This is impressive because math can get a little boring and repetative at times. I also like this class because the grading system isn't as bad as other class.

1. Chemistry

This is my favorite class because of three main reasons. The first reason is the teacher. Mr. Nebens may seem scary at times but he is actually really nice to his class. He also teaches the subject really well.

The second reason is that chemistry is really intresting. We do alot of labs and all of them were fun to do with my friends. Overall, Chemistry is the best class I am currently in and I find it really fun.
