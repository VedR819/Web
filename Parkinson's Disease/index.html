About The Author

Hello, my name is Ved Raorane. At the time of writing this, I am a student at SSHS (Santa Susana High School). This is a project for my Exploring Computer Science class. I wrote about Parkinson's Disease because of my personal passion. I've always loved learning about neurology, so studying for this website was really fun.

Personal passion out of the way, this website is constructed into three segments. The first segment is about the problem relating to Parkinson's Disease. Dopamine and symptoms of Parkinson's Disease is in this segment. The second segment is about the solution. The treatments available for Parkinson's Disease will be described in this segment.
